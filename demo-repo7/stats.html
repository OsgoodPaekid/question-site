Mr Nobody

The man who lived a good life

Here's a time line of Mr Nobody:

  • 1955 - Born at Kojo Krom

  • Started school at Kwamekrom in 1957

  • Moved from Obokrom to continue his education at Kojo Ntum

  • Completed JHS in 1974

  • Married in 1983

  • First born is a female and is 32 years now

  • Second born also a female and is 26 years now

  • Third born and forth are twins and boys

  • And the last born is also a boy

  • Mr Nobody was a farmer and was very diligent in his work.

  • In the year 2000, he moved to a different village to continue a new farm there

  • He was a good leader in Church and at home too. He was the pioneer of the Church Of Pentecost in Kojo Ntum

  • He was part of the first leadership of the church's new District when it was first formed

  • Mr Nobody died in the year 2012 after some months of illness

"Mr Nobody's life is one that's worth emulating. He was very good to people of all walks of life and also a disciplinarian that takes church and religious life very serious"
